The Lost Kitten

One rainy afternoon, a little gray kitten sat shivering under a bush. Sarah saw the kitten on her way home from school. "Oh, you poor thing!" she said softly. She knelt down and held out her hand. The kitten slowly came closer and rubbed against her leg. Sarah carefully picked up the kitten and wrapped it in her jacket. She carried it home and showed her mom. "Can we keep it, please?" Sarah asked. Her mom smiled and said, "Let's put up some posters first. Someone might be looking for this kitten." Sarah was sad but agreed. The next day, a girl named Emma came to the door. "That's my kitten, Whiskers!" she exclaimed. Sarah felt happy that Whiskers was going home, but she would miss the little kitten.
