A3 · Describe Characters

"Describe the characters in the story."
The Brave Little Turtle

In a peaceful pond, there lived a little turtle named Toby. Toby was small but very brave. While the other turtles hid in their shells when danger came, Toby would peek out to see what was happening. His best friend was a bright blue dragonfly named Dara. Dara was quick and clever. She would fly high above the pond and warn everyone about approaching storms. One day, a big heron came to the pond looking for food. All the turtles hid, but Toby stayed to help Dara, who had hurt her wing. "Don't worry, Dara," Toby said. "I'll protect you." Toby carried Dara on his back to a safe hiding spot under the lily pads. The other turtles watched in amazement. After the heron left, they all cheered for Toby. "You are the bravest turtle we know!" they said. Toby smiled and said, "I just helped my friend."
