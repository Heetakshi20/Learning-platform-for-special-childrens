A4 · Compare and Contrast

"Compare and contrast the two items."
Apples
🍎🍏

Apples grow on trees. They can be red, green, or yellow. Apples are sweet or tart. You can eat them fresh or bake them in pies. Apples have seeds inside.

Oranges
🍊🍊

Oranges grow on trees too. They are orange and have a thick peel. Oranges are sweet and juicy. You can eat them fresh or drink orange juice. Oranges have seeds inside.

Compare and Contrast: Apples vs. Oranges
Category Apples Oranges
Where they grow on trees on trees
Colors red, green, yellow orange
Have seeds yes yes
