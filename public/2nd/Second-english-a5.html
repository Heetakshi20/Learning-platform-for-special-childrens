What is a Whale? 🐋

Whales are mammals that live in the ocean. They breathe air through blowholes on top of their heads.

Types of Whales

There are two main types: baleen whales and toothed whales. Baleen whales have bristles in their mouths to filter food. Toothed whales have teeth to catch fish.

Did You Know? The blue whale is the largest animal on Earth! It can grow as long as three school buses.
Whale Songs

Male whales sing beautiful songs that can travel far through the water. Each whale has its own special song.
