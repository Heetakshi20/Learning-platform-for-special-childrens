B3 · Prefixes and Suffixes

"Add prefixes and suffixes to make new words."
What are prefixes and suffixes?
  • 👉 Prefix: letters added to the beginning of a word that change its meaning
  • 👉 Suffix: letters added to the end of a word that change its meaning
Prefixes
un- unhappy, unlock (not happy, open lock)
re- redo, replay (do again, play again)
pre- preview, preheat (view before, heat before)
mis- mistake, misplace (wrong take, wrong place)
Suffixes
-ful helpful, playful (full of help, full of play)
-less fearless, hopeless (without fear, without hope)
-er teacher, player (one who teaches, one who plays)
-ly quickly, slowly (in a quick way, in a slow way)
