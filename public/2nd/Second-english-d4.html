"Where does the apostrophe go?"
How to Use Apostrophes
  • 👉 Contractions: show missing letters (don't = do not)
  • 👉 Possession: show something belongs to someone (Sam's ball)
Contractions
do not → don't
I am → I'm
it is → it's
Possession
Sam → Sam's ball
dog → dog's bone
teacher → teacher's book
___ is my favorite toy.
